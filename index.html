Safety by design

QuickMove does not show either party’s phone number.

The parking problem is simple. The resolution process is not.

When someone parks in a private or assigned spot, the rightful spot user often has only uncomfortable options: wait, confront, call property management, or escalate to towing.

⏳

Waiting feels helpless

Residents can lose time waiting for a driver, property manager, or tow truck to resolve a private parking conflict.

⚠️

Direct confrontation is risky

Knocking on doors, leaving notes, or confronting a stranger can create safety and emotional friction.

🚗

Towing becomes the first escalation

For drivers, a mistake can become expensive. For property teams, it can create complaints and disputes.

QuickMove creates a documented middle step.

QuickMove is a privacy-first communication and documentation layer that gives both sides a structured chance to resolve the issue before escalation.

MVP position: QuickMove does not replace property rules, towing policies, permits, emergency procedures, or legal enforcement.

How the MVP works

The MVP tests the happy path while protecting both the driver and the scanner from misuse.

Vehicle registers

Driver enters phone, plate/state, VIN, vehicle details, consent, and insurance-card verification.

Spot user reports

Scanner uses QR or plate/state, verifies phone, selects a structured parking issue, and submits.

Masked alert sent

QuickMove sends one platform-controlled alert with no direct phone-number sharing.

Driver responds

Driver can move now, request five minutes, dispute, or report misuse through structured options.

Active alert lock

During the 10-minute window, additional reports for the same vehicle are saved but no new alerts are sent.

Resolution timer

The scanner sees a waiting window and can confirm the vehicle moved or proceed to escalation.

Incident record

The system saves timestamps, response status, resolution outcome, and escalation status.

Trust scores update

Driver Trust, Scanner Trust, and Case Confidence are updated from outcomes — not one-sided claims.

Trust and abuse prevention are core product features.

QuickMove makes one respectful, documented alert easy — and repeated, suspicious, or aggressive contact difficult.

🛡️

MVP guardrails

No direct calls, no free-form messages, phone OTP for scanners, no registration-status reveal, one active incident window, rate limits, and dispute/abuse reporting.

🔒

Privacy by default

QuickMove should not reveal phone numbers, owner identity, scanner identity, VIN, insurance document, address, or whether a plate is registered.
